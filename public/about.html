About this site

This site was first published on October 19, 2024. At that point, it was v5.0 of my personal site. v1.0 was originally deployed on Februrary 22, 2024, but it was located at a different domain than it is today.

Back then, the page ran directly out of a Cloudflare Pages setup, using a subdomain on pages.dev. In 2025, it was relocated to a "real" domain, amethystx.net!

My original intent for making this website is unclear. Maybe escapism, maybe popularity, maybe reflecting on my existence. At some point in the process however, I fell in love with the Internet. Not news outlets, not followers, not stock markets, not user content, but just the Net on its own. There's more creative freedom in the information-transporting systems enmeshing our planet than any medium I've ever used. Amazingly, so much of the beauty of the Net is hidden behind the scenes, the kind of awesome shit that you only find if you go out of your way to look for it.
