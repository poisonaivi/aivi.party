not actually a party

hi, i'm aivi she/it, a self-taught web dev & furry artist. i pride myself in creating things with my own two paws, and i despise commercial social media platforms, so i built this website to share my work with the world on my own terms.


most of everything here was inspired, at least in part, by other people's creations. i encourage you to view and adapt my code or otherwise take inspiration from this site. You can also use whatever you'd like from my asset library.


even though this site is 2 years, 4 domains, 6 redesigns, and probably over a thousand commits old, it still is, and will always be, a work in progress.
